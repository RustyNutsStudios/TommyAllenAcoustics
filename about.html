>> THE SHORT VERSION

I'm Tommy Allen. I have an MSc in Acoustics and a passion for video games. I make little browser-based audio tools because I find them interesting, and sometimes I make actual video games too.

This site is a messy hub for all the mini projects I build. Some are useful. Some are weird. All of them run entirely in your browser.
